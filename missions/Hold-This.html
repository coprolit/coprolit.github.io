Intel

We have seized a key strategic position. Hold it until relieved.

Setup

During setup, before deployment, you place an objective marker in your deployment zone (or table edge). Just before beginning the first turn of the game, after all deployment is finished (including snipers, spotters and observers), designate a non-vehicle unit to defend the objective.

Victory

At the end of the game, if you completed your objective succesfully you treat a defeat result in the scenario being played as a draw, and a draw result as a victory.

In case both players have succeeded their objective, the missions are disregarded when working out which side has won, drawn or lost the scenario.
